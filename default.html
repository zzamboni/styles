About

If you were looking for the typical "about me" page, this is it. There's probably more interesting stuff to see (or not) elsewhere around here. You've been warned.

I (Diego Zamboni) am a computer scientist living in Mexico with my wife Susana and our daughters Karina and Fabiola. We recently moved back to Mexico from Switzerland, where I worked at the IBM Zurich Research Lab, and before that we lived in the U.S., where I studied at Purdue University, I still have a web page there.

I currently work at HP Mexico as an Outsourcing consultant. My main fields of knowledge are computer security, virtualization and configuration management.

I was born in Argentina, but have moved around all my life. When I was very young I moved to Mexico, where I lived in 4 different cities before moving to the U.S. to pursue my Ph.D. After that, we decided to go to Switzerland. After 8 years, we decided to come back to Mexico.

So there. Maybe some more later when I'm more inspired.

If you are interested, here's my curriculum vitae. For other useless trivia about me, see here.

I had a blog called BrT which I closed when I moved my blogging to the wonderful Posterous. I still keep a static copy of it for reference.

Diego Zamboni // Computer security wonk, Mac addict without a Mac, husband, dad.
